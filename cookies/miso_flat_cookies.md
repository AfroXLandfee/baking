# Maple Miso Milk Chocolate Cookies

> **Prep time:** 15 minutes | **Chill time:** 30-45 minutes | **Bake time:** 15-18 minutes
> **Bake Temp:** 375°F

---

## Ingredients

* **210 g** (1⅔ cups) bread flour
* **160 g** (¾ cup) unsalted butter, *melted and slightly cooled*
* **140 g** (⅔ cup, packed) brown sugar
* **110 g** (½ cup) granulated sugar
* **40 g** (2 tbsp + 1 tsp) white miso
* **30 g** (1½ tbsp) maple syrup
* **1** large egg
* **1** egg yolk
* **1 tsp** vanilla extract
* **3 g** (about ½ tsp) baking soda
* **3 g** (1 tsp) cornstarch
* **4 g** (1 tsp) salt
* **140 g** (1 cup) milk chocolate, *chopped*
* **Flaky salt**, *for topping*

---

## Instructions

### Step 1: Mix the Wet Ingredients
1.  In a large bowl, **combine** the slightly cooled melted butter with the brown sugar and granulated sugar.
2.  **Add** the white miso, maple syrup, and vanilla extract. Mix until well incorporated.
3.  **Whisk in** the egg and the egg yolk.

### Step 2: Combine Wet and Dry
1.  In a separate bowl, **whisk** together the dry ingredients: bread flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt.
2.  **Fold** the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until *just* combined. Be careful not to over-mix!
3.  **Stir in** the chopped milk chocolate.

### Step 3: Portion and Chill
1.  **Scoop** out the cookie dough. *(Tip: For giant cookies, weigh them to 170g each. This yields about 5-6 cookies).*
2.  **Top** each dough ball with a sprinkle of flaky salt.
3.  **Chill** the portioned dough in the refrigerator for **30-45 minutes**.

### Step 4: Bake and Cool
1.  **Preheat** your oven to **375°F**.
2.  **Bake** the chilled cookies for **15-18 minutes**.
3.  Let the cookies **cool on the pan** for **10 minutes** before transferring them to a wire cooling rack for an additional **10-15 minutes** to set completely.
