# Red Velvet Cookies with Cream Cheese Bark

> **Prep time:** 20 minutes | **Chill time:** 2.5 - 4 hours | **Bake time:** 15-18 minutes
> **Bake Temp:** 350°F

---

## Ingredients

### Cream Cheese (CC) Bark
* **113 g** (½ cup) cold cream cheese
* **40 g** (⅓ cup) powdered sugar
* **20 g** (2½ tbsp) cornstarch
* **50 g** (¼ cup) white chocolate, *melted and cooled*
* **5 g** (1 tsp) vanilla extract
* **1 pinch** of salt

### Red Velvet (RV) Cookie Dough
* **355 g** (2¾ cups) all-purpose flour
* **35 g** (about ⅓ cup) cocoa powder
* **6 g** (1 tsp) baking soda
* **2 g** (½ tsp) cream of tartar
* **3 g** (½ tsp) kosher salt
* **4 g** (1½ tsp) cornstarch
* **227 g** (1 cup / 2 sticks) unsalted butter, *cold and cubed*
* **200 g** (1 cup, packed) brown sugar
* **120 g** (⅔ cup) granulated sugar
* **1** large egg
* **2** egg yolks
* **10 g** (2 tsp) vanilla extract *(Mexican vanilla is recommended!)*
* **5 g** (1 tsp) liquid red food coloring
* *Optional:* **50–100 g** (⅓–½ cup) white chocolate chunks

---

## Instructions

### Step 1: Make the Cream Cheese Bark
1.  **Mix** all the bark ingredients together in a bowl until fully smooth.
2.  **Spread** the mixture onto a parchment-lined baking sheet into an even ½–¾ inch layer.
3.  **Freeze** for **30–60 minutes** until solid.
4.  **Chop** the frozen bark into chunks and return them to the freezer to stay cold while you make the dough.

### Step 2: Prepare the Red Velvet Dough
1.  **Whisk** together the dry ingredients (flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, cream of tartar, kosher salt, and cornstarch) in a medium bowl. Set aside.
2.  **Mix** the cold, cubed butter and both the brown and granulated sugars until *just* combined. Be careful not to over-mix.
3.  **Add** the egg, egg yolks, vanilla extract, and red food coloring. Mix until just combined.
4.  **Incorporate** the dry ingredients into the wet mixture half at a time, mixing until just combined.
5.  **Fold** in the frozen cream cheese bark chunks and the optional white chocolate chunks.

### Step 3: Chill and Bake
1.  **Freeze** the prepared cookie dough for **2-3 hours**.
2.  **Preheat** your oven to **350°F**.
3.  **Bake** the chilled dough for **15-18 minutes**. You want the edges to be set while the centers remain soft. Let cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
